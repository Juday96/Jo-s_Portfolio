About Me

Hi! I’m a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate who loves solving problems and creating things that make people’s lives a little easier.

Over the years, I’ve gained hands-on experience in IT support, troubleshooting, and system configuration, helping others overcome both hardware and software challenges.

I enjoy combining creativity and technology to build something that truly makes an impact.

I enjoy blending technical skills with creativity — whether it’s designing something visually engaging or building solutions that simply work better.

I’m also familiar with tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office, which help me bring ideas to life in both technical and creative ways.

At the heart of what I do is a passion for innovation, learning, and helping others through technology — because I believe that great digital solutions start with understanding real people and their needs.
